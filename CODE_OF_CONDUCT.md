# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, considerate, and patient.
- Use inclusive language and assume good faith.
- Give and receive constructive feedback graciously.
- Credit the work of others and acknowledge mistakes.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Harassment and exclusionary actions are not tolerated. Examples include:
- Personal attacks, hateful or derogatory language.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or other identity.
- Threats, stalking, or sustained disruption of project activities.
- Publishing private or identifying information without consent.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers at: [INSERT EMAIL] or open a confidential issue labeled "code-of-conduct" on the project repository. Include:
- A description of the incident with dates, locations, and relevant context.
- Contact information and any evidence (screenshots, links, logs).

All reports will be handled promptly and with discretion.

## Enforcement
Consequences may include:
- A private warning.
- Temporary or permanent removal of communication privileges.
- Removal from project roles or contribution access.
Decisions will be made by the maintainers and may involve other contributors as needed.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (code, issue trackers, chat, mailing lists, events) and to behavior at project-related social events.

## Acknowledgements
This document is intended to foster a healthy community. Maintainers may update it as the project evolves.
